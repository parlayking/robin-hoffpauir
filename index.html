This is a collection of my baseball analysis, machine learning models, and predictive algorithms.

Finding my calling in the coding space was easy, and to be honest, pretty obvious had I given it much thought. Seeing as I played baseball through College and my dreams of making it to the pros would never be realized. This didnt stop me from keeping my finger on the pulse of the league and all the games nuances. Last year when I started my coding journey, I knew fairly quickly, what I would be diving into. Although I was nprepared for how much data is out there, especially in baseball with the statcast revelation in the mid 2000's
